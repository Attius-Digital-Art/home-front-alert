Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Home Front Alert (the "App") is a life-saving tool designed to provide real-time missile and security alerts. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information Collection

Location Data: The App requires access to your Precise Location (GPS) in the background. This is used ONLY to calculate your distance from active rocket alerts and trigger appropriate warning tones. This location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT uploaded to our servers.

Usage Data: We do not track personal identifies or browsing history.

2. Data Usage

Your location is used solely to provide real-time distance-based alerts. We do not sell, share, or store your location history.

3. Background Processing

Because missile threats require immediate attention, this app may run a background service to ensure you receive alerts even when the screen is off. You can disable this in the app settings.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: homefrontcommand@attius.com
