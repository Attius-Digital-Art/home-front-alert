Home Front Alert / התראות פיקוד העורף

Important: This application monitors alerts for all of Israel, not just your specific location. It uses distinct audio patterns instead of standard alarms to allow "eyes-off" awareness. Because of its country-wide scope, the app will be noisy to reflect the actual number and state of alerts across all alerted areas. It does not replace the official Home Front Command app for individual safety.
